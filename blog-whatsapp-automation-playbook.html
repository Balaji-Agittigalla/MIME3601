WhatsApp Automation Playbook for Cart Recovery & Repeat Sales

Category: Retention · 7 min read · Updated April 2026

For many D2C brands, WhatsApp outperforms email on open and response rates. The key is message sequencing and segmentation.

Core Flows to Implement

  • Abandoned cart reminders with dynamic product context.
  • Order and delivery updates to reduce support load.
  • Post-purchase cross-sell flow based on bought SKU.
  • 90-day winback flow for inactive customers.

Message Strategy

Keep messages short, relevant, and timed. Use clear value statements and one focused CTA per message.

Avoid Common Mistakes

  • Over-messaging without segmentation.
  • Same copy for first-time and repeat buyers.
  • No tracking for campaign-level ROI.
