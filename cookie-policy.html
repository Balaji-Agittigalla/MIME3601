Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies are, how MIME360 uses them, and how you can control cookie preferences.

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and measure usage.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To improve site performance and reliability
  • To understand usage behavior and optimize pages
  • To support third-party integrations when needed

3. Cookie Categories

Essential Cookies

Required for core website functionality such as page navigation, form processing, and security-related behavior.

Analytics Cookies

Used to understand anonymous traffic patterns and improve performance, content flow, and UX decisions.

Functional Cookies

Store interface choices like language or interaction preferences where available.

Third-Party Cookies

May be set by embedded tools or communication platforms (for example form handlers and messaging integrations).

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect feature availability and site experience.

For regions where consent is required for non-essential cookies, cookie preferences should be captured before those cookies are set.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect website or legal changes.

6. Contact

If you have questions about our cookie usage, contact hello@mime360.com.
