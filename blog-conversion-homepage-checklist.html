Homepage Conversion Checklist for Service & eCommerce Brands

Category: CRO · 6 min read · Updated April 2026

Your homepage should answer three questions quickly: what you do, who you help, and why visitors should trust you.

Above-The-Fold Structure

  • Strong headline with clear business outcome.
  • One primary CTA and one secondary CTA max.
  • Short supporting copy with benefit-first language.

Trust & Proof

Add logos, quick case wins, testimonials, and measurable claims. Place these before heavy feature descriptions.

Friction Points to Remove

  • Unclear navigation labels.
  • Overcrowded hero sections.
  • Form fields that ask too much too early.

Mobile First Check

Most users arrive on mobile. Prioritize thumb-friendly CTA placement, fast load speed, and scannable spacing.
