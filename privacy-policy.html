Privacy Policy

At Mime360, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe.

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact form, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Company name
  • Project details or messages submitted through the contact form

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and contact requests
  • Discuss project scope, timelines, and services
  • Improve website performance, user experience, and service quality

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers used to run our business workflows (for example form handling, email, or messaging tools), only as needed to process your request.

4. Third-Party Tools

Our site may rely on third-party tools such as Formspree for form delivery and messaging tools such as WhatsApp for communication. Those services process data under their own privacy terms.

Form submissions on this website are handled via Formspree endpoint integration.

5. Data Security & Retention

We use reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect your data against unauthorized access or misuse. We keep data only as long as needed for business communication, legal obligations, and service delivery.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us directly. If required by applicable laws in your region, we also support opt-out and consent withdrawal requests.

7. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy over time. Any updates will be published on this page with the latest effective date.

8. Contact

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@mime360.com.
