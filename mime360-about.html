Our Origin Story

FROM ENTERPRISE
TO EVERYONE.

Our experience comes from working on large-scale Shopify projects within established agencies — contributing to the development and growth of high-performance e-commerce stores. That’s where we learned what truly works at scale.

But one thing became clear. High-quality websites were treated as a luxury. Small businesses, emerging D2C brands, and solo founders were often priced out before they even had a chance to compete.

Mime360 was built to change that. We apply the same performance-driven approach used in large projects — without unnecessary overhead or inflated costs. The focus is simple: build fast, scalable, and effective digital experiences that actually help businesses grow.
